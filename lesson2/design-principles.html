Design Principles on well-designed websites

PARC: Alignment

Site: Fallout of Bethesda

Url: https://fallout.bethesda.net/en

Description: I see alignment all around this site. Even the text on the picture is aligned. The yellow paragraph is centered with the text below it, the yellow buttons are also centered. All that alignment creates order and makes easier to understand the content of the website.

White Space and Clean Design

Site: Dribbble

Url: https://dribbble.com/

Description: I love how easy it is to see the content of this website. I think it is because of the padding and margin that the site has. You can see in the navbar white space on all four sides of each element, the hamburguer menu, the logo, and the Sign in button. You can also find the white space during all the site.

PARC: Proximity

Site: Ferrari

Url: https://www.ferrari.com/en-US

Description: When I visit this site, I don't get lost while reading all its content. I think it is because of the proximity of its elements. You can apreciate in the picture how the title, picture and description is one next to eachother. This resource of proximity, makes possible for people to understand that this elements are related and makes it easier to understand what is going on on the website.
