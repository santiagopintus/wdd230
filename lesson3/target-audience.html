Target Audience

  • Description: Our target audience is people from 12 to 100 years old. They have active lives and care about the climatical conditions. The target audience may have a full-time job or a long day in school and some sportive activities. Usually, they depend on the weather to perform some exercises. The income of the user doesn't matter since this is going to be a costless app. The objective is to be an intuitive use website, so the user doesn't need to have any experience using weather websites or apps. The user may access the website in a hurry, just before leaving home or some roofed place. So, the most common device will be a mobile device.

  • Personas
      • Persona 1
      • Name: Sandra Jones
      • Age: 48 years old
      • Yearly income: $64000
      • Education: Bachelor's in Human resources
      • Experience with weather apps: Every day, Sandra needs to know about the weather. That is
        • because she and her three children need to go out and prepare for the weather conditions.

      • Persona 2
      • Name: Robert Clayton
      • Age: 13 years old
      • Yearly income: $3000
      • Education: Elementary School
      • Experience with weather apps: Three times a week, Robert takes swimming classes in an outdoor swimming pool. If it rains, he does not have classes. He likes to be self-reliant, so he consults the weather and prepares himself according to the climatic conditions.
      • In what conditions he visits the site? Robert is a bit forgetful. Most of the time, he opens the weather site in a hurry. Luckily, he knows the website has the information right at hand, and his tablet is always on.
      • Goals: He wants to compete in the Olympics and win a gold medal. For him, there is nothing better than swimming.
